Self Portrait in a Straw hat

By Louise Élisabeth Vigée Le Brun

The painting appears, after cleaning, to be an autograph replica of a picture, the original of which was painted in Brussels in 1782 in free imitation of Rubens's 'Chapeau de Paille', which Vigée Le Brun had seen in Antwerp.It was exhibited in Paris in 1782 at the Salon de la Correspondance.Le Brun's original in a private collection in Frace.
